Tortillas are a mexican cuisine that have been in the world for hundreds of years.

Its a very simple recipe that only takes a few minutes tom prepare and can be enjoyed with a side of dip or salsa.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 to 2 cups oil for frying

  2. 12 corn tortillas cut into 6 wedges each

  3. Flaky sea salt

Instructions:

  • Heat the oil until the temp. reaches 350F then reduce the heat to medium

  • Fry the tortillas for 2 and 3 minutes, until they are lightly golden and crispy

  • Drain the excess oil

  • Serve with a side of dip or salsa

  • Enjoy
